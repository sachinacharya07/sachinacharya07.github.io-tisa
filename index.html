🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026
What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and profile photo. We store your study data, todos, assignments, journals, timetable, and badge progress in Firebase — a secure Google service.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your email address for marketing, phone number, location, or any payment information. We do not run ads. We do not sell your data to anyone.

How your data is stored

All data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. Your personal study data (todos, journals, assignments) is private and only accessible by you. Public profile info like your username and study hours are visible to other TISA users you connect with.

Community features

Forum posts and chat messages you send are visible to other logged-in TISA users. Please don't share sensitive personal information in public posts.

Your rights

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Delete My Data" button in Settings. You can also sign out and stop using the app at any time.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, email us at tisa.helpdesk@gmail.com or reach out via the TISA community forum.
